Life at NJS Public School

Campus Sports Infrastructure

The sports facilities at our institution are designed to bridge the gap between amateur recreation and professional athletic training. By integrating high-end equipment with expansive outdoor arenas, we provide a sanctuary for physical health and mental clarity. Our facilities include a weather-resistant 400-meter track, multi-sport courts for basketball and tennis, and a specialized aquatic wing. These spaces are more than just physical assets; they are the heart of our community where students cultivate leadership, teamwork, and the discipline required to excel in both sports and academics. Every surface, from the low-impact gym flooring to the floodlit football turf, is maintained to the highest safety standards to ensure an injury-free environment for all participants.

Educational Excursion: Hastinapur

The school is proud to announce an upcoming excursion to the ancient city of Hastinapur, a site of immense historical and cultural significance. This trip is designed to offer students a firsthand look at the remnants of the Kuru Empire and the rich spiritual heritage of the Jain temples. To ensure a seamless experience, the school has organized top-tier trip facilities, including secure transportation in certified tourist buses, healthy buffet-style meals, and accommodation in vetted hospitality partners. Our itinerary balances educational lectures at historical sites with recreational visits to the local wildlife sanctuary, all under the vigilant supervision of our senior teaching staff and on-site medical coordinators.

Our Achievements

Creative & Extra-Curricular Life

The school’s commitment to a well-rounded education is best seen through our immersive clay activities and diverse club offerings. In our pottery workshops, students engage in the meditative practice of sculpting, using high-quality terracotta and stoneware clay to bring their imaginations to life. This tactile experience is complemented by a wide array of other activities, ranging from high-energy sports tournaments to intellectual debates and cultural festivals. By participating in these programs, students learn to step outside their comfort zones, fostering a spirit of innovation and resilience. Whether they are perfecting a glaze in the kiln room or programming a robot for a regional competition, our students are constantly encouraged to explore, create, and lead.
